Introduction

Hello and welcome to my website, which was originally designed for a university assignment. The project had me attempting to bridge the gap between what I can show on my CV and what has been posted on social media. All that information has been gathered into one place to show a picture about who I am and what I've achieved on my journeys.

I've never been one to enjoy talking about themselves, and this website should help show more about myself than any words I say ever could. I aim to update this site with more content as time goes on and I have more to show under my belt. This website is designed to reflect my choices and as such the colours, font and simple design is all as inteded - matching my terminal environment I use on a daily basis.
